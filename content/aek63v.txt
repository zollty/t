<p>题干=</p><p>Java并发编程里面有哪些常用的API？至少说出3种</p><p>答案=</p><ul class=" list-paddingleft-2" style="list-style-type: disc;"><li><p>集合：ConcurrentMap、CopyOnWriteArrayList、BlockingQueue等</p></li><li><p>原子数据：AtomicLong、AtomicBoolean等</p></li><li><p>锁：ReentrantLock、ReentrantReadWriteLock（tryLock,unlock）</p></li><li><p>同步：CountDownLatch（countDown,wait）</p></li><li><p>线程：ExecutorService，ScheduleExecutorService，Executors（newFixedThreadPool, newCachedThreadPool, newSingleThreadExecutor，newScheduledThreadPool&nbsp;）</p></li></ul><p><br/></p>
